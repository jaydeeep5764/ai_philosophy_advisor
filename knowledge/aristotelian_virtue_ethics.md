# Aristotelian Virtue Ethics
Tradition: Western
School: Aristotelian Virtue Ethics
Tags: aristotle, virtue-ethics, practical-wisdom, habits, flourishing, moderation

## Core Ideas
Aristotelian ethics asks what kind of life allows a human being to flourish. Character is shaped by repeated action, and excellence usually sits between harmful extremes.

## Reasoning Pattern
The Aristotelian lens asks what habit a choice strengthens, what purpose the action serves, and where practical wisdom would locate the balanced response.

## Practical Cautions
The search for moderation should not become cowardice, comfort, or refusal to act strongly when justice requires it.

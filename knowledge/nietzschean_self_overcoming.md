# Nietzschean Self-Overcoming
Tradition: Western
School: Existentialism / Continental Philosophy
Tags: nietzsche, self-overcoming, values, individuality, strength, herd-mentality

## Core Ideas
Nietzschean reasoning challenges inherited values, passive conformity, resentment, and weakness disguised as morality. It pushes the person to create values and transform difficulty into strength.

## Reasoning Pattern
This lens asks whether the user is acting from fear, herd approval, resentment, or a deeper creative strength.

## Practical Cautions
Self-overcoming is not ego worship. Strength without honesty, responsibility, or creative discipline becomes vanity.

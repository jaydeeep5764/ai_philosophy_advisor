# Platonism
Tradition: Western
School: Platonism
Tags: plato, platonism, forms, justice, reason, education, soul

## Core Ideas
Platonism asks the mind to rise beyond appearances toward truth, justice, order, and the deeper form of things. The soul is better governed by reason than appetite or ambition.

## Reasoning Pattern
The Platonic lens asks what ideal is being served, what illusion is misleading the user, and whether the choice orders or disorders the soul.

## Practical Cautions
Idealism can become abstraction or elitism if it loses contact with lived reality and ordinary human needs.

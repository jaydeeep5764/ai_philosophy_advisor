# Arthashastra Strategy
Tradition: Eastern
School: Arthashastra / Political Strategy
Tags: chanakya, arthashastra, statecraft, strategy, governance, economics, security

## Core Ideas
The Arthashastra tradition treats leadership and social order as practical disciplines involving resources, incentives, information, security, and enforcement.

## Reasoning Pattern
This lens asks what outcome is needed, what constraints exist, who has leverage, and what plan preserves stability without confusing wishes for strategy.

## Practical Cautions
Strategic realism becomes dangerous when it loses ethical limits, compassion, or concern for the people affected by decisions.

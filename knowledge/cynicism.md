# Cynicism
Tradition: Western
School: Cynicism
Tags: diogenes, cynicism, simplicity, anti-materialism, freedom, virtue

## Core Ideas
Cynicism challenges artificial needs, status games, luxury, vanity, and dependence on public approval. It values freedom through simplicity and self-sufficiency.

## Reasoning Pattern
The Cynic lens asks what is actually necessary, what is social performance, and whether the user is trading freedom for admiration.

## Practical Cautions
Rejecting convention can become irresponsibility or contempt. The useful target is false pride, not every human bond or duty.

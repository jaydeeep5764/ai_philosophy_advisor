# Taoism
Tradition: Eastern
School: Taoism
Tags: laozi, taoism, wu-wei, simplicity, humility, nature, non-forcing

## Core Ideas
Taoism emphasizes harmony with the Tao, simplicity, humility, flexibility, and action that does not force reality against its grain.

## Reasoning Pattern
The Taoist lens asks where striving is creating resistance, where softness may be stronger than force, and what action fits the natural pattern of the situation.

## Practical Cautions
Non-forcing is not doing nothing. It is timely, minimal, intelligent action without anxious overcontrol.

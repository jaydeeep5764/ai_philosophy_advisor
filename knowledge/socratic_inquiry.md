# Socratic Inquiry
Tradition: Western
School: Socratic Philosophy
Tags: socrates, socratic-method, inquiry, self-knowledge, virtue, assumptions

## Core Ideas
Socratic philosophy uses questions to test definitions, reveal contradictions, and turn attention toward virtue and self-knowledge.

## Reasoning Pattern
The Socratic lens asks what key terms mean, what assumptions are unexamined, and whether the user is mistaking confidence for wisdom.

## Practical Cautions
Questioning should lead to clearer living. It can become avoidance if the person never chooses or acts.

# Confucianism
Tradition: Eastern
School: Confucianism
Tags: confucius, confucianism, virtue, ritual, duty, family, social-harmony

## Core Ideas
Confucianism emphasizes cultivated character, humane conduct, ritual propriety, family responsibility, education, and social trust.

## Reasoning Pattern
The Confucian lens asks how a choice affects relationships, duties, example-setting, and the moral fabric of a community.

## Practical Cautions
Harmony should not become blind obedience. Good order depends on humane conduct, not mere hierarchy or appearances.

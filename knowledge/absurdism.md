# Absurdism
Tradition: Western
School: Absurdism
Tags: absurdism, meaning, revolt, freedom, solidarity, camus

## Core Ideas
Absurdism begins from the tension between the human hunger for meaning and a world that does not clearly provide final answers. A lucid response does not deny the tension and does not collapse into despair.

## Reasoning Pattern
The absurdist lens asks whether the user is escaping into false certainty, nihilism, or distraction. It emphasizes honest attention, chosen responsibility, and action without guarantees.

## Practical Cautions
Absurdism should not become theatrical hopelessness. Its healthier form supports revolt, freedom, dignity, and solidarity with others.

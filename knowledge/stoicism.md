# Stoicism
Tradition: Western
School: Stoicism
Tags: marcus-aurelius, stoicism, discipline, duty, control, virtue

## Core Ideas
Stoicism separates what is within one's control from what is not. It values virtue, duty, disciplined judgment, and emotional steadiness under pressure.

## Reasoning Pattern
The Stoic lens asks what depends on the user's choices, what must be accepted, and what action would preserve character.

## Practical Cautions
Stoicism is not emotional numbness. It should train wise response, not denial of grief, fear, or human need.
